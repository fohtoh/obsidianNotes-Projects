## Study Plan

### Monday: What is the Old Testament?
*   **Read:** "Introduction to the Old Testament" from the Come, Follow Me manual.
*   **Ponder:** What are your feelings about studying the Old Testament? What are some of the challenges and blessings of studying this book of scripture?
*   **Activity:** Watch the video "The Old Testamentâ€”a Testament of Jesus Christ" (if available on the church's website).

### Tuesday: The Covenant Path
*   **Read:** The section "The Covenant Path" in the "Introduction to the Old Testament" lesson.
*   **Ponder:** What is a covenant? What covenants have you made with God? How does the Old Testament teach you about God's covenants with His children?

### Wednesday: Symbols of Christ
*   **Read:** The section "Symbols of Christ" in the "Introduction to the Old Testament" lesson.
*   **Ponder:** How can you look for symbols of Christ as you study the Old Testament? How can these symbols strengthen your faith in Him?

### Thursday: The Law of Moses
*   **Read:** The section "The Law of Moses" in the "Introduction to the Old Testament" lesson.
*   **Ponder:** What was the purpose of the law of Moses? How did it point people to Jesus Christ?

### Friday: Personal Study
*   **Read:** Choose a chapter from the Old Testament that you are not familiar with.
*   **Ponder:** What did you learn from your reading? How does this chapter testify of Jesus Christ?

### Saturday: Family Discussion
*   **Discuss:** Share with your family or a friend what you have learned this week about the Old Testament.
*   **Activity:** Set a goal as a family or individual to study the Old Testament regularly this year.

### Sunday: Reflect and Apply
*   **Ponder:** How can you apply what you have learned this week to your life? How can you make your study of the Old Testament more meaningful this year?
